# Landing Page Direction

Role: supporting product design reference.

## Retained Copy Direction

The page uses a mix of light manifesto, product clarity, and community tone.

Goal: stay memorable without losing immediate clarity.

- Badge: `TOOLSTR · NOSTR FRANCOPHONE`
- Hero: `Moins de friction. Plus de liens. Un Nostr francophone plus accessible.`
- Description: `Pars avec les bons reperes : un starter pack francophone, des profils Nostr utiles, puis des outils pour avancer sans repartir de zero.`
- Final block: `ToolStr commence simple. Pas petit.`

The tone avoids both startup-heavy and overly technical language. It stays direct, community-oriented, and compatible with an evolving home page.

## Product Direction

The page presents ToolStr as a francophone Nostr toolbox, not only as a starter-pack access page.

The starter pack remains the main currently available module. It should stay visually primary while the structure remains ready for future modules.

Future modules considered for home presentation without overselling availability:

- `Fusion des listes`: in construction, to consolidate Nostr lists.
- `Onboarding Nostr`: in construction, dimmed while content is not ready.
- `Profils recommandes`: interesting but later in the roadmap.

## Layout Direction

- Short hero to establish the product promise.
- Module grid to show what exists and what can arrive.
- Final block to explain the extensible toolbox logic.

## Visual Direction

- Keep the brutal grid background.
- Keep white cards with black borders for clean reading.
- Keep `Disponible` highly contrasted for the priority action.
- Reserve orange for the `Starter pack francophone` module.
- Reserve light gray for WIP/dimmed modules.
- Keep the main badge white to calm the top of the page.

The target is softer brutalism: less aggressive, but still distinctive.

## Future Iteration Rules

- Keep the current home as the base.
- Do not return to a yellow/orange-heavy full-page palette.
- Avoid too many cards; this version depends on breathing room.
- Every new module needs explicit status: `Disponible`, `Actif`, `Bientot`, or equivalent.
- Do not oversell future modules.
- Keep the starter pack as the first flagship module while it remains the main path.
